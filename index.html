+Every Attack (Punch, Kick, Combo)-Every Miss (No Contact/phantom)

What is Fight proof?

A Player vs Player mobile game built to run on a fork of the TRON Network through its multihash algorithm called the TRON Protocol. The TRON Protocol consists of Smart Contracts running on the TRON Virtual Machine and a combination of the following hash algorithm: sha2-256, sha2-512 and sha3. F!GHTproof is bringing it back old school with its arcade style of 1-on1 gameplay like that of classic titles, Street Fighter and Mortal Kombat, but with a modern twist! F!GHTproof is to feature not only location-based gameplay with geo-location tagging of digital assets but also those same digital asset scan be viewed through your mobile device in a real world with the use of augmented reality. But wait, it gets even better... F!GHTproof's main attraction is its F!GHT-N!GHT Ecosystem which includes the Match Wager System, Match Explorer, and the Leaderboard. Players will now have the ultimate bragging rights of saying, "Put your money where your mouth is!"

Are there different versions of your TKO Token?

Yes. Our TKO token will first be available via our Token Generation Event. This token is a TRC-20 Token based on the TRON Network. It will be our TRON version, hence the name TTKO. However, our TTKO Token will eventually be swapped out for TKO, our mainnet token, once we get our very own blockchain we call Katharos, up and running.
